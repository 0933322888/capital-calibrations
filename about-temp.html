About Our Company

Capital Calibrations was founded to meet the growing demand for accurate, OEM-compliant ADAS calibrations within the collision repair industry. As modern vehicles rely increasingly on advanced driver assistance systems, proper calibration has become essential to ensure both vehicle safety and repair integrity.

Our mission is simple: provide reliable, professional calibration services directly at your facility, helping collision centers and repair shops complete repairs efficiently without sending vehicles off-site.

Capital Calibrations operates as a mobile calibration service, bringing advanced diagnostic and calibration equipment directly to your shop. This approach helps reduce delays, keep vehicles within your repair workflow, and accelerate delivery times for your customers.

Every calibration we perform follows OEM procedures and industry best practices. Vehicle ride height, tire pressure, alignment specifications, and environmental conditions are all critical factors that must be correct before calibration begins. By ensuring these variables are addressed, we help deliver accurate and repeatable results for every vehicle.

Using advanced ADAS calibration equipment and modern diagnostic tools, Capital Calibrations is equipped to service a wide range of vehicles and driver assistance systems including radar, camera, blind spot monitoring, and surround view technologies.
